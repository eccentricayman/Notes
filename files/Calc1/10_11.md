## Exponential Functions

- f(x) = a^x^ where a != 0

## Base

## Change of Base Formula

- log~a~b = log~c~b / log~c~a

## Natural Logs

- log~e~x = ln(x)

## Properties of Logs

- ln and log are interchangeable.
- ln(1) = 0
- ln(ab) = ln(a) + ln(b)
- ln(a/b) = ln(a) - ln(b)
- ln a^x^ = x * ln(a)
- log~a~(a) = 1
- ln e^x^ = x
- e ^ln(x)^ = x
